Brandy's Background

I was born and raised in Houston, Texas and have not known much else for most of my life. My rough background gave me the skills in life I needed to follow my passions no matter what. I graduated in 2013 and have always had a passion for programming. That passion grew as I endeavored to compete in coding challenges in high school. I began taking Computer Science as a Sophomore and completed Computer Science I-III through to my senior year.

In the professional world I have done Insurance work for almost 6 years now and am licensed in the state of Texas but on the side I always knew it was just temporary until I could find a way to make my software engineering dream become a reality. I began to research different certifications and schooling that would teach me more than just earning a degree. I wanted to be able to apply myself in a way that went beyond the average teaching context.
